Phanerozoic Eon
539 Mya – Today
The Phanerozoic is our present geologic eon. During this time, diverse and complex life has spread across Earth, including the evolution of human beings.
Proterozoic Eon
2500 – 539 Mya
During the Proterozoic, oxygen became a major component of Earth’s atmosphere and multicellular life began to become common.
Archean Eon
4031 – 2500 Mya
During the Archean, the solar system finished its formation and the first life on Earth arose.
Hadean Eon
4567 – 4031 Mya
During the Hadean, Earth's surface cooled after its initial formation. The Moon was formed after a massive collision between Earth and a Mars-sized planet.
